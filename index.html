Frequently Asked Questions


How long does it take?
Most regular loads are ready within 6 hours. However, with heavy loads it would be ready within 24 hours.


Do you offer pickup and delivery?
We unfortunately do not have this service because of our limited resources.


What if an item gets damaged?
We treat every item with care, but if an item is damaged, we provide full repair and give refunds.


What are your operating hours?
We are open Monday to Friday from 9:00 AM to 7:00 PM, and Saturdays from 9:00 AM to 2:00 PM.
